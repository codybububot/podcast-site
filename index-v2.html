EP 02 Coming Soon

Can the Fortune Teller Read His Own Fortune?

Levy Cheng was rejected by nearly 100 investors. They said AI fortune-telling was a scam. He built FateTell anyway — and tens of thousands now pay for AI-generated life reports based on ancient Chinese wisdom. But in this conversation, we skip the mystical jargon. We ask: Does HE believe in fate? What are people actually paying for when the charts are free online? When someone changes their life based on an AI reading, whose responsibility is that? And if FateTell fails — would he call it destiny?
